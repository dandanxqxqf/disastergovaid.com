SBA Coronavirus (COVID-19) Small Business Guidance & Disaster Loan Resources

 

About SBA Disaster Loans : The U. S. Small Business Administration (SBA) provides low-interest, long-term disaster loans to businesses of all sizes, private non-profit organizations, homeowners, and renters to repair or replace uninsured / under-insured disaster damaged property. SBA disaster loans offer an affordable way for individuals and businesses to recover from declared disasters.

See the official SBA website to try and do this yourself, or save your valuable time and contact us directly to help you navigate through this complex and confusing SBA loan process.

All 50 states have now been declared disaster areas due to the Coronavirus (COVID-19), with $350 billion available in grants, forgivable loans and low interest loans through the SBA.
