SBA Loan Consulting

Save your valuable time. Money can always be replaced. Let us help you determine which SBA Grant and Loan Financial Resources will best suit your small business needs, assist you in all SBA application processes and speak with the SBA on your behalf.

ERC Filling

Our Team is exclusively dedicated to understanding and maximizing the CARES Act Employee Retention Credit (ERC) for U.S. based businesses that may have been negatively impacted by COVID-19.

Although the basic qualifications for the ERC program may seem easy, the understanding of each qualifier can be very challenging. Our experienced Team was designed to help accurately access your eligibility before guiding you successfully through filing for your refunds while maximizing on any qualifications that may be available to your company. 

SSBCI GRANT

Disaster Government Aid is a company that provides assistance to businesses affected by disasters. They are currently involved in helping businesses apply for the State Small Business Credit Initiative (SSBCI) grant. This grant provides funding to eligible small businesses to help them grow and create jobs. Disaster Government Aid assists businesses with the application process and ensures that they meet all the eligibility criteria. By working with Disaster Government Aid, businesses can access the resources and support they need to thrive and recover from disasters.

 SBA Paycheck Protection Program (PPP)

Disaster Government Aid helps small businesses with SBA PPP loans by providing resources, guidance, and services. They offer information on eligibility, loan amounts, and application procedures, as well as SBA loan advising and filing services to assist with completing the application and ensuring documentation is in order. With Disaster Government Aid's help, small business owners can secure the SBA PPP loan and keep their businesses running.

Do you need a small business loan/grant?

Disaster Government Aid provides loan resources and services to assist individuals and businesses. They offer comprehensive information on loan types, interest rates, and repayment terms, as well as guidance on improving credit scores and preparing loan applications. Disaster Government Aid also offers loan advising services to review loan terms and negotiate with lenders. With their assistance, borrowers can secure favorable loans and keep their finances on track.

Restaurant Revitalization Fund (RRF)

Disaster government aid can assist your business for the Restaurant Revitalization Fund (RRF) by providing help with applications, offering technical expertise, coordinating with other organizations, advocating for the restaurant industry, and enabling struggling businesses to access the resources they need to recover.
