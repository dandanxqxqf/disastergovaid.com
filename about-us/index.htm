About us

Honesty. Integrity. Responsibility. Dedication. Compassion.


Disaster Government Aid is a trusted Team of Professionals dedicated to saving small businesses from lost sales, lost customers, lost revenue and rescuing your business from potential financial ruin.

Our Mission

It’s our mission to help every small business owner, sole proprietor, solopreneur and entrepreneur, navigate through these rough uncharted waters during this unprecedented historical crisis and time of need.


$350 Billion in Emergency SBA Funds were made available by the US Federal Government as part of the $2 trillion stimulus package signed into law on March 27th, 2020, for small businesses with less than 500 employees.

Let us help you determine which U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) Grant and Loan Resources will best suit your present business needs, assist you in the complex application process, and speak with the SBA on your behalf, to help ensure you have the greatest chance of success in getting approved versus doing this on your own.
